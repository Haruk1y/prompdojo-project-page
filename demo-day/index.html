Concept

More AI, More Party!

PrompDojo is a brand-new party game powered by generative AI. Players infer the prompt behind a generated image, and the player who creates the closest match wins.

How To Play

See, Write, Generate

  1. 1
    Study the image

    Subject, background, color, composition, style... every detail matters!

  2. 2
    Write the prompt

    Turn what you notice into words the AI can draw from.

  3. 3
    Generate with AI

    Create your best shot.

  4. 4
    Compare the match

    The closer it is to the target, the higher your score!
